SOULSDLE

Try to guess the Dark Souls weapon using clues

How to play

Enter your guess

A correct attribute is green

An incorrect attribute is red

A partially correct attribute is blue

The summary shows what correct attributes you've discovered

At 7 and 3 remaining attempts you can give up one attempt to use a clue that will reveal one correct attribute in the summary
